About Me

I am a former Aeronautical engineer turned software engineer with a passion for building things that are sustainable and user-focused. I have experience working with JavaScript, React, Redux, Ruby on Rails, Node.js, SQL, and MongoDB, in addition to HTML and CSS. My current project is a single-page app that allows users to buy and sell product online. Ruby on rails is used as web AWS S3 storage for an interactive and scalable application. As a former quality officer, I love working on cross-functional teams, collaborating with coworkers, and balancing maintainability with moving quickly and producing concrete results.
